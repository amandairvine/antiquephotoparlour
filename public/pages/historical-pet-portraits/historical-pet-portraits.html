    Tips For The Perfect Portrait:

    ▼
    • Ensure your pet is in focus with good lighting.
    • A close-up of their face
      and upper body works best.
    • Have them looking at the camera or gazing off in the distance.
    • Avoid blurry photos or
      images taken from far away.

    Feel free to send multiple photos
    so we can choose one
    that best fits
    the pose and outfit!

  5. 5. Let the Magic Begin

    Your regal pet portrait will be ready to admire and show off in one week!
