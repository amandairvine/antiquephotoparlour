What Does the Sitting Fee Include?

▼

Sitting Fee includes:

  • Outfits, Accessories, and Props

    • For groups over 8 people,
      2 reposes are included.
      Additional reposes are $20.00 each.
  • One Unframed 8x10 Portrait

Pricing

How long is a sitting?

How long is a sitting?

▼

The length of a sitting depends on
many factors, such as:

  • Number of People

    • For example, the average 2 Person sitting takes about 30 to 45 minutes.

      Dressing, shooting, and proofing
      can take time.

  • Chosen Theme

    • Some themes have options that we can easily fashion over top of your clothing.

      Other themes may require clothing to be removed.

  • Busy Hours

    • At peak times, we may have more than one group in the studio.

      We appreciate your patience and will always make sure every group gets the
      attention they deserve.

  • Children

    • Dressing little ones can sometimes take extra patience.

      Please allow a little extra time if your group includes children.

How many people can you fit
in a photo?

How many people can you fit in a photo?

▼

For the best results, we recommend bringing in 12 people or less.

Due to the size of our studio, any group larger than 12 tends to be quite crowded.

If you have a larger party, we suggest splitting into smaller groups.

Feel free to contact us regarding larger group sizes and we would be happy to make suggestions.

Are babies considered people?

What About Babies and Toddlers?

▼

We love capturing those precious early years!

Pricing for photos in our studio is
the same no matter your age.

See Pricing Here

We have a few suggestions to ensure
a smooth and enjoyable
experience when photographing little ones:

  • Scheduling:

    • We highly recommend scheduling your photo session around your
      little one’s nap
      and meal-time schedule.

      A well-rested and fed baby is a
      happy baby!

  • Clothing and Props:

    • We have a selection of small outfits and props specifically for our tiniest clients.

      If you have a special outfit or prop you'd like to use, feel free to bring it in!

      We can also provide simple wraps and blankets if you'd prefer.

For examples of our baby, toddler, and child photography, see the Kids section of our Themes page:

Kids Photos Here

Do you provide hairstyling
or makeup?

Hair Styling and Makeup Application:

▼

Our wonderful photographers
do not provide hairstyling
or makeup application.

Please ensure that you come in with your hair and makeup done how you would like
it to be in your photos!

If you plan to visit our studio after a
fun-filled day at West Edmonton Mall’s
World Waterpark, please ensure hair is dry and you’re prepared to be on camera!

Note: We do have a hair dryer if necessary!

If you have any questions about what is supplied at the studio, please contact us!

Contact Us

Are pets allowed in the studio?

Are pets allowed in the studio?

▼

All members of your family are welcome for your portrait!

We’ve photographed a vast array of animals in our studio,
including cats, dogs, snakes, parrots, and bunnies.

You are welcome to bring in your own
outfits for your pets if you would like.

Note: We may not have clothing or accessories
that will fit your pet.

See examples of our pet photos in the Pets section of our Themes page:

View Pet Photos

If you are planning on bringing your
pet to the studio, please contact us
ahead of time.
