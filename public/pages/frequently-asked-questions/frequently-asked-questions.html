What does a sitting look like?

What To Expect During a Sitting:

Once you've chosen a theme and
enter our studio's dressing area,

your sitting will begin!

1. Clothing Selection

We will show options for clothing based off of the theme that you chose.

Depending on the outfit you choose and the clothing that you are wearing when you visit us, we may require you to change using one of our two curtained-off changing rooms.

Note: Although not required, we recommend wearing similar attire to the following for the smoothest experience:

Ladies: Black or White Tank Top under easily removable layers with Shorts or Leggings.

Gentlemen: T-Shirt or Tank Top under easily removable layers with Black Trousers
(Either Jeans or Dress Pants - if there are logos/designs/side pockets or they are differently coloured, we will provide Black Pants for you)

Once you’ve checked out your outfit and everyone in your group is happy with how they look, we’ll bring you to our set for your photos!

Note: We provide bins for you to store your items in, such as bags, purses, jackets, sweaters, shoes, etc. Those bins will stay with your group while you take your photos so you can keep an eye on them!

2. Photography

We’ll set the scene to match your chosen theme, including posing!

Note: The size of your group and your chosen outfits will impact posing. Please talk to your photographer about any alternate poses you have in mind, and we’ll decide if we can accommodate or make alternate suggestions. Got a good side? A pose you love or one you don’t? Let us know! We’ll do our best to guide you and shoot in ways that play to your strengths. Our goal is to help you look and feel your best.

Once posed, we'll hand out props for everyone to hold.

Note: Our staff reserves the right to deny props to any persons we deem unfit to properly handle our property at any time during a sitting. This includes children of all ages as well as adults! We also have age restrictions on some props, due to weight, size, and risk of injury, such as our replica swords, rifles and pistols. However, we do have many alternate props for your youngsters! Ask us for further details.

Then, the fun begins! Your photographer will instruct you throughout your sitting on expressions, where you should look, and fun theme-related activities using your provided props!

3. Photo Proofing

Once your photo session is completed, you’ll be brought back to the dressing area to return to your modern day attire.
From there, your group will make their way to our computers so we can go through your photos with you.

We’ll help you narrow down your favourite photos and provide suggestions for prints and digital copies!

The Sitting Fee for your group includes one printed 8x10 photo.
For additional prints and digital copies, see our Pricing page for details.

How long is a sitting?

How long is a sitting?

The length of a sitting depends on
many factors, such as:

  • Number of People

    • For example, the average 2 Person sitting takes about 30 to 45 minutes.

      Dressing, shooting, and proofing
      can take time.

  • Chosen Theme

    • Some themes have options that we can easily fashion over top of your clothing.

      Other themes may require clothing to be removed.

  • Busy Hours

    • At peak times, we may have more than one group in the studio.

      We appreciate your patience and will always make sure every group gets
      the attention they deserve.

  • Children

    • Dressing little ones can sometimes take extra patience.

      Please allow a little extra time if your group includes children.

How many people can you fit in a photo?

How many people can you fit in a photo?

For the best results, we recommend bringing in 12 people or less.

Due to the size of our studio, any group larger than 12 tends to be quite crowded.

If you have a larger party, we suggest splitting into smaller groups.

Feel free to contact us regarding larger group sizes and we would be happy to make suggestions.

What About Babies and Toddlers?

We love capturing those precious early years!

We have a few suggestions to ensure a smooth
and enjoyable experience when photographing little ones:

      A well-rested and fed baby is a
      happy baby!

  • Clothing and Props:

      If you have a special outfit or prop you'd like to use, feel free to bring it in!
