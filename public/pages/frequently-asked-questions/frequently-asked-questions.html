Frequently Asked Questions

What does a sitting look like?

Once you've chosen a theme and we bring you into our studio's dressing area, your sitting begins!

1. Clothing Selection

We will show you options for your outfits based off of the theme that you chose.

Depending on the outfit you choose and the clothing that you are wearing when you visit us, we may require you to change using one of our two curtained-off changing rooms.

Note: Although not required, we recommend wearing similar attire to the following for the smoothest experience:

Ladies: Black or White Tank Top under easily removable layers with Shorts or Leggings.

Gentlemen: T-Shirt or Tank Top under easily removable layers with Black Trousers (either jeans or dress pants - if there are logos/designs/side pockets or differently coloured, we will provide black pants for you)

Once you’ve checked out your outfit and everyone in your group is happy with how they look, we’ll bring you to our set for your photos!

Note: We provide bins for you to transport your things in, such as bags, purses, jackets, sweaters, shoes, etc. Those bins stay with your group for your whole sitting!

How long does a sitting take?

A typical photo sitting takes between 1-2 hours, depending on the number of outfit changes and the complexity of the theme you've chosen.

Can we wear our own outfits?

Absolutely! You're welcome to bring your own outfits. We also have a wide selection of themed costumes and accessories available in our studio.

Do we have to make an appointment?
How many people can we bring in?

Yes, appointments are required to ensure we can give you our full attention. We can accommodate groups of up to 6 people for most sessions.

What sizes do we carry?

We carry a full range of sizes from XS to 3XL in most of our costume selections. We want everyone to feel comfortable and confident during their sitting.

Do you do hair or makeup?

We offer basic styling and touch-ups. For more elaborate looks, we can recommend professional hair and makeup artists who work with our studio.

Do you allow pets?

We love including furry family members! Pets are welcome with advance notice so we can prepare the studio appropriately for a safe and comfortable experience.

Can you edit our photos?

Yes! Basic editing and retouching are included with all packages. We can discuss additional editing services if you have specific requests.
